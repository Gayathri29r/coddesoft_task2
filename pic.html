ABOUT US..

Cozy Café is a popular coffee shop in Mumbai, India.
The coffee shop started in 2008 and has continued to grow and expand ever since.
The café is known for its delicious coffee, baked goods, and tex-mex menu.
The coffee shop combines its food and beverage offerings with art, entertainment, and special events
like weekly bonfires. It’s known as one of the best cafes in Mumbai.
